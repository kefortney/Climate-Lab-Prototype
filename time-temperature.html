Time & Temperature

Goal: Use heat precisely to transform ingredients and ensure food safety—while minimizing energy waste.

Learn & Try

  • Match cooking methods (sauté, roast, braise) to ingredients and desired textures.
  • Use thermometers and carryover heat to hit doneness safely.
  • Choose equipment and settings to save energy (batching, lids, residual heat).
