About This Non‑Credit Site

This site adapts ideas from the University of Vermont’s Climate Kitchen—an initiative that uses the kitchen as a collaborative makerspace to link cooking, sensory learning, and sustainability—to support cooks of all kinds.

The Climate Kitchen’s teaching integrates five sustainability tenets across curricula, workshops, and research, and emphasizes hands‑on, inquiry‑based learning.
