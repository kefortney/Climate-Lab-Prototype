Sequence & Planning

Goal: Map backwards from “serve” time, then sequence tasks to cook efficiently and reduce stress and waste.

Learn & Try

  • Build a grid plan for cooking multiple dishes in parallel.
  • Design a plan for someone else (e.g., a family member) with their constraints.
